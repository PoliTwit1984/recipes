Perfect Prime Rib

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 3-4 hours Serves: 8-10

Ingredients

  • 1 (5-7 lb) prime rib roast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Remove the prime rib from the refrigerator 2-3 hours before cooking to bring it to room temperature. This ensures even cooking.

  2. 2.

    Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).

  3. 3.

    In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.

  4. 4.

    Rub the roast all over with olive oil, then coat evenly with the herb mixture, pressing it into the meat.

  5. 5.

    Place the roast in a roasting pan, fat side up. Insert an oven-safe meat thermometer into the thickest part of the meat.

  6. 6.

    Roast at 450°F for 15 minutes to sear the outside. Then reduce the temperature to 325°F (165°C) and continue cooking until desired doneness.

  7. 7.

    For medium-rare (recommended), cook until internal temperature reaches 120-125°F (49-52°C), about 15 minutes per pound.

  8. 8.

    Remove from oven and let rest for 15-20 minutes before carving. The temperature will rise 5-10 degrees during resting.

Pro Tips

  • Choose a roast with good marbling for the best flavor
  • Let the meat rest after cooking to retain its juices
  • Slice against the grain for maximum tenderness
